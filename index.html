About Me

Hello! I'm Negar Akhgar, currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science at the University of Milan. I specialize in data analysis and enjoy uncovering patterns and insights from complex datasets.

With a strong foundation in computer science and a passion for data, I have developed skills in various data analysis tools and programming languages, including Python, and SQL. My coursework and projects have equipped me with the ability to handle large datasets, perform statistical analyses, and create data visualizations that provide actionable insights.

My interest in data analytics stems from the belief that data-driven decisions can significantly improve business outcomes. I aim to leverage my skills to help organizations make informed decisions, optimize processes, and drive growth.

This website is a platform where I share my experiences, insights, and resources related to data analysis. Whether you're a fellow data enthusiast or someone curious about the field, I hope you find the content here useful and inspiring.
